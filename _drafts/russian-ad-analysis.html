When I first posted the Russian Ad Explorer on Reddit, someone commented something that's stayed on my mind for the last month.

Idk what your political leaning is but if the US truly is compromised it is up to the people to coordinate a defense. This data is an analysis of Russia's attack on Western Democracy and you are doing the work of a patriot, whether you are American or not, regardless of your political background. Thank you, sincerely.
[reddit]

As nice as it was, it made me feel a little off. When I first heard of the dataset, I was interested in it because it showed, in a kind-of perverse way, what non-Americans thought of Americans. Even a quick scroll through the publicly-released version of the dataset showed ads targeting almost every highly-visible sect of American life (except for Asian Americans), and the click statistics showed that those ads were successful. I was interested in learning what the Russians had figured out about people living in this country &emdash; what worked and what didn't.
